About

Danielle is a full stack developer in training and an Agile Certified Product Manager/Owner (ACPMPO®) specializing in Scum and Kanban methodology. Danielle describes her career by stating, ever had that light bulb moment or what Oprah likes to call it the “ah ha” moment!? For Danielle, her “ah ha” moment came at the height of her career when she realized she had an affinity for leadership, people, and technology. So, after an amazing decade in the behavior health sector Danielle decided to change careers. Many thought Danielle’s idea was insane but after choosing to change careers, she has landed several awesome AWESOME positions with Verizon. Currently, Danielle works as a Product Owner/Solution Architect building vision and prioritizing AI/ML technologies that create personalized customer experiences. Danielle is very happy to say she loves to lead, while bridging people and technology together but being a woman in tech is much more invaluable!
